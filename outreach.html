Outreach Activities

As an undergraduate I was involved in the local, Lancashire and Cumbria, branch of the Institute of Physics. This involved going into primary schools putting on a short play called Galactic Gig, followed by providing hands on demonstrations of the physics of light and sound.

Chalkdust

Currently, I am one of the editors of Chalkdust magazine. I have written an article about Skyrmions for Chalkdust alongside my collaborator Bjarke Gudnason. I also occasionaly set the cryptic as Skyrmionium.
