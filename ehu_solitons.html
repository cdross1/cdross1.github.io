Topological Solitons at Edge Hill

On July 10th 2024 I will host a one day event about topological solitons at Edge Hill University funded by an LMS celebrating new appointments grant. The day will consist of approximately four talks about topological solitons and related topics.

Confirmed participants

The invited speakers are

  • Guido Franchetti, University of Bath
  • Derek Harland, Unversity of Leeds
  • Tom Winyard, University of Edinburgh

If you would like to attend please send me an email at calum.ross[at]edgehill[dot]ac[dot]uk, there is, very, limited funding available to cover some transport costs for speakers and any PhD students who wish to attend. Please get in touch if you would like ask about this support.
